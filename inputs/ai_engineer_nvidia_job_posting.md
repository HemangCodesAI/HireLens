# AI Engineer

## About NVIDIA

NVIDIA is a world leader in artificial intelligence, pushing the boundaries of what's possible with GPUs and AI software. We're dedicated to innovating across diverse sectors, including gaming, data centers, autonomous vehicles, and more. Join us to be at the forefront of the AI revolution and impact the world with your groundbreaking work.

## Job Overview

We're looking for a passionate and driven AI Engineer to join our team. You'll be responsible for developing, implementing, and deploying cutting-edge AI models and solutions. Your work will involve tackling complex problems, collaborating with a team of talented engineers, and contributing to our mission of pushing the boundaries of AI.

## Responsibilities

*   Develop and implement AI/ML models for various applications using deep learning and machine learning techniques.
*   Collaborate with a team of engineers to design, build, and deploy AI solutions.
*   Work with large datasets, applying data preprocessing and feature engineering techniques.
*   Conduct experiments to optimize model performance and accuracy.
*   Stay up to date with the latest AI trends, technologies, and research.
*   Contribute to the development of tools and infrastructure for AI development.
*   Document your work and provide technical guidance to other team members.

## Required Skills and Qualifications

*   Master’s or Ph.D. degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, or a related field.
*   Strong understanding of machine learning and deep learning algorithms and concepts.
*   Proficiency in Python and AI libraries such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, and scikit-learn.
*   Experience with building and training deep learning models (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers, etc.).
*   Experience with data analysis, data preprocessing, and feature engineering.
*   Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
*   Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
*   Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
*   Familiarity with cloud computing platforms like AWS, GCP, or Azure is a plus.
*   Experience with GPU computing and CUDA is a significant advantage.
